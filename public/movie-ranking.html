The Best Movies According to Ashley

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Truman Show

This is one of my favourite films. I like the way it plays on the idea that Jim Carreys life being a tv show.
Everyone he interacts with is an actor and he is basically living on a large movie set.

Ex Machina

One of my favourite movies about artificial intelligence.

Bleach: Fade to black

Bleach is one of the best animes in my opinion.
